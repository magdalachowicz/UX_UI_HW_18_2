Skills

User Empathy

The baseline. A thorough understanding of a client’s mission, their competition, and current user base inform my approach and what tools to utilize.

Research + Inquiry

Of what use are boots on the ground if you don’t know whose shoes you’re wearing? Understanding users elevates design beyond its appearance, adds depth to the user experience, and creates room for further growth.

Adaptable Design

Speaking to users, both current and potential, filters out any noise from the research and provides detailed insights into the direction that my work should take. I don’t seek the correct answers, but where the answers may lead.
